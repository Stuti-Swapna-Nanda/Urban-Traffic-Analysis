# 🚦 Urban Traffic Analysis of Bangalore

## 📌 Project Overview

This project focuses on analyzing urban traffic patterns in Bangalore to identify congestion hotspots, traffic trends, and environmental impact using data analytics and visualization techniques.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

* Analyze traffic volume and trends over time
* Identify high congestion areas and roads
* Study traffic incidents based on location and weather
* Evaluate environmental impact due to traffic conditions
* Build an interactive dashboard for better decision-making

---

## 📊 Features

* 📈 Traffic Volume Analysis (Year-wise trends)
* 🚧 Congestion Analysis (Top congested roads & areas)
* 🚗 Average Speed Monitoring
* ⚠️ Incident Analysis by Area & Weather
* 🌱 Environmental Impact Analysis
* 📍 Area-wise comparison using interactive visuals

---

## 🧠 Key Insights

* Koramangala is identified as the peak congestion area
* Sony World Junction shows the highest congestion among roads
* Environmental impact increases with traffic volume
* Cars contribute the highest to environmental impact
* Certain areas consistently show high traffic density

---
